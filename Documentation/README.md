# E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes e-commerce sales data to understand revenue trends,
customer behavior, product performance, and operational challenges.

An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to convert raw sales data
into business insights and actionable recommendations.


## Business Objective

The objective is to answer key business questions:

- How is revenue performing over time?
- Which products contribute the most revenue?
- Are customers returning or only making one-time purchases?
- What factors impact business growth?


## Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- DAX
- Data Modeling
- Excel / CSV
- Python (Data Cleaning)


## Dataset Overview

The dataset contains transaction-level information including:

- Orders
- Customers
- Products
- Revenue
- Discounts
- Dates
- Order Status


## Dashboard Structure


### Page 1: Executive Overview

Provides a high-level business performance summary.

Key metrics:

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Average Order Value

Visuals:

- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Category Revenue Contribution
- Business Performance Summary


### Page 2: Product Analysis

Focuses on product-level performance.

Analysis includes:

- Top performing products
- Revenue contribution
- Product comparison
- Category trends


### Page 3: Customer Analysis

Analyzes customer behavior.

Includes:

- Customer distribution
- Repeat vs new customers
- Revenue dependency on customer groups


## Key Insights

Examples:

- Revenue is concentrated among a few products.
- Customer retention needs improvement.
- Discounts have a strong impact on sales.
- Certain categories require growth strategies.


## Recommendations

- Improve customer retention campaigns.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-value customers.
- Optimize discount strategies.
- Monitor low-performing products.
